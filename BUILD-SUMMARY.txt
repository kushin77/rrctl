INSTALLATION
------------

Quick install:
  curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kushin77/elevatedIQ/main/rrctl-opensource/install.sh | bash

Manual:
  1. Download binary for your platform from rrctl-opensource/
  2. chmod +x rrctl-*
  3. Move to /usr/local/bin/ or add to PATH

USAGE EXAMPLES
--------------

Analyze repository:
  rrctl repo-defrag --path . --json report.json --md report.md --plan plan.md

Auto-fix workflows:
  rrctl repo-autofix --path . --dry-run --patch fixes.patch

Apply fixes:
  rrctl repo-autofix --path . --dry-run=false

With GitHub API:
  rrctl repo-defrag \
    --path . \
    --github-owner your-org \
    --github-repo your-repo \
    --github-token $GITHUB_TOKEN
